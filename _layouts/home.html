About DataCareTeam

Who we are
  • We are a team of data experts working in the fields of science and technology. We developed our careers at CERN, the largest particle physics laboratory in the world, located in Geneva (CH). We participate in world-leading projects in a mission to study the fundamental constituents of matter, such as the Higgs boson discovered in 2012.
  • The technologies developed at CERN have a major impact on the world, as it happened in the 1990s with the World Wide Web. In recent years, we have been leading the way in the big data revolution. Our research involves handling a large amount of data and analysing it with cutting-edge data science techniques.
  • In our free time, we enjoy helping non-profit organizations that pursue goals that we care about with their data-related tasks. These activities can often be performed remotely, and we can therefore support organisations from every corner of the world.

Our volunteers can carry out data analysis projects related to health, environment, education, society and more.
